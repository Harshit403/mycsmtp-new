Comprehensive Guide to CS Professional Test Series

Introduction to CS Professional Examination

The CS Professional exam conducted by the Institute of Company Secretaries of India (ICSI) represents the final and most challenging stage of the Company Secretary course. This examination tests candidates on advanced aspects of corporate governance, strategic management, and professional skills required for practicing as a qualified Company Secretary. Our CS Professional Test Series is meticulously designed to help aspirants conquer this crucial final hurdle with confidence and competence.

Understanding the CS Professional Program Structure

The CS Professional program is divided into three comprehensive modules that test advanced knowledge and practical skills:

Module I: Governance, Risk Management, Compliances and Ethics

  • Advanced corporate governance principles and practices
  • Risk management frameworks and implementation
  • Compliance management systems
  • Business ethics and corporate sustainability

Module II: Strategic Management, Applied Business Laws and Economics

  • Strategic decision making and implementation
  • Financial and treasury management
  • Applied business and corporate laws
  • Economics for business strategy

Module III: Drafting, Pleadings and Appearances

  • Drafting various legal and commercial documents
  • Appearances before various authorities and tribunals
  • Corporate restructuring documents
  • Due diligence reports and certifications
