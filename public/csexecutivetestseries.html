Comprehensive Guide to CS Executive Test Series

Introduction to CS Executive Examination

The CS Executive exam conducted by the Institute of Company Secretaries of India (ICSI) is a crucial professional examination that tests candidates on various aspects of corporate laws, governance, and management. This intermediate stage of the Company Secretary course bridges the gap between the foundational CSEET and advanced CS Professional levels. Our CS Executive Test Series is meticulously designed to help aspirants navigate this challenging phase with confidence and competence.

Understanding the CS Executive Program Structure

The CS Executive program is divided into two modules, each containing four papers that comprehensively test different aspects of corporate governance and management:

Module I:

  • Jurisprudence, Interpretation and General Laws
  • Company Law
  • Setting up of Business Entities and Closure
  • Tax Laws

Module II:

  • Corporate and Management Accounting
  • Securities Laws and Capital Markets
  • Economic, Business and Commercial Laws
  • Financial and Strategic Management
